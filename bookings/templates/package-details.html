Cox's Bazar Beach Retreat

Best Seller Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh 3 Days, 2 Nights 4.8 (120 reviews)

Overview

Experience the world's longest natural sea beach with our expertly crafted Cox's Bazar retreat package. This 3-day adventure offers the perfect blend of relaxation and exploration, allowing you to enjoy the stunning beaches while discovering the local culture and attractions.

Our experienced local guides will ensure you experience the best of Cox's Bazar, from the most picturesque spots on the beach to hidden gems only locals know about. With comfortable accommodations, delicious meals, and hassle-free transportation, this package is perfect for couples, families, and solo travelers alike.

Package Highlights

  • Stay at a premium 4-star beachfront hotel
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Bay of Bengal
  • Visit Inani Beach and its coral reef
  • Experience a sunset boat ride
  • Explore Himchari National Park
  • Savor fresh seafood and local Bengali cuisine
  • Shop for local handicrafts and souvenirs

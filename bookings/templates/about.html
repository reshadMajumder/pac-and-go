Our Story

Pac-and-Go was born from a simple idea: travel should be more than just visiting placesâ€”it should be about truly experiencing them. After years of traveling the world and finding the most meaningful experiences came from connections with local guides, our founder decided to create a platform that made these authentic experiences accessible to everyone.

We started small in 2021 with just a handful of destinations and guides. Today, we've grown to include hundreds of carefully vetted tour guides across dozens of countries, but our mission remains the same: connecting travelers with authentic, unforgettable experiences.

What sets us apart is our commitment to both travelers and guides. We believe in fair compensation for our guides and affordable, transparent pricing for our travelers. Every package on our platform is created by local experts who are passionate about sharing their corner of the world.

As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to our core values of authenticity, sustainability, and community impact. We're proud to have helped thousands of travelers create memories that will last a lifetime, while supporting local economies and promoting responsible tourism practices.
